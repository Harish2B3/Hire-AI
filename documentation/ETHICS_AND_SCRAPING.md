# Legal and Ethical Guidelines

- Use only public information.
- Respect robots.txt, terms of service, rate limits, and platform policies.
- Avoid collecting sensitive personal data unless it is clearly public and relevant.
- Keep API keys server-side.
- Do not spam contacts or automate unsolicited outreach.
- Provide opt-out and human review for any future communication automation.
- Store only normalized hiring intelligence needed for the application.
